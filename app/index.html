Frequently Answered Questions

Still considering? Let us help you with some frequently asked questions.

  • Why choose us?

    You would have the opportunity to work with best professional personnel, as well as practice through real life projects. Work alone or as a group, you would enhance your skills and create projects for your portfolio with best practices and techonologies. Either you have worked your web skills before or not, you would be comfortable with web concepts after your graduation

  • Program requirements

    There are no pre-requirements to enter our program. All you need is to bring your best self and be eager to learn web concepts and principles, as well as continuesly practice through real life projects and homework.

  • Do I need special equipmet?

    You do not need any special equipment in order to follow our program. In our campus you will find top edge computers with all the necessary software. However in order to practice and complete our project a personal computer is highly recommended. Our campus will help you supply with all the software you will need.

  • Program Fees

    You are very welcome to come to our Campus to discuss about fees. Fees depend on the time you register to our program, as well as the installments you choose. Send us your e-mail and we will inform you about the next available Open day you can come to see all our programs, our facilities and our personnel.
